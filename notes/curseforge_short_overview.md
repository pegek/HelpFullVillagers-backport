Helpful Villagers (1.12.2) transforms standard Minecraft villages into bustling, self-sufficient communities with their own dynamic economy and working citizens. Originally created for 1.7.10, this comprehensive backport introduces 11 fully functional professions, advanced custom AI, and rich village infrastructure.

Instead of merely wandering and trading, villagers now actively contribute to the town's survival. The mod features 11 unique professions including Lumberjacks, Miners, Farmers, Fishermen, Ranchers, Builders, Merchants, Soldiers, Archers, and the newly added Cleric. Each profession has complex AI routines: Miners dig branching tunnels to find ores, Builders construct and demolish structures using special boundary fences, and Farmers actively harvest crops.

Village defense has been completely redesigned from the ground up. Soldiers and Archers actively patrol the village, employing smart targeting (like ignoring creepers to prevent structural damage) and utilizing shields for blocking. When injured below 50% health or out of ammunition, guards will intelligently retreat to their Guild Halls to deposit loot and restock their armor, weapons, and arrows from chests. The new Cleric profession acts as the village healer and enchanter. By converting mob drops into "essence" at a brewing stand, Clerics can throw splash potions to heal wounded villagers, cleanse negative debuffs, and even bless and fully repair the guards' equipment using an enchanting table based on kill milestones.

To organize your workforce, you can establish Guild Halls by simply placing an item frame with a profession-specific tool next to a door. The mod dynamically scans the interior of the building, allowing villagers to claim chests and furnaces for their work. Furthermore, the village operates on a fully dynamic economy that calculates item prices based on a scan of the local abundance of resources and crafting recipes, constantly adapting to supply and demand.

With built-in anti-stuck mechanisms, flawless multiplayer compatibility, and sophisticated pathfinding, Helpful Villagers breathes genuine life and purpose into your Minecraft world!
